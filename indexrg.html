How to Complete Your Calories & Protein Goals

Fulfilling nutrition goals is crucial for physical transformation. Follow these REPNOXX guidelines:

Completing Calories

Veg Focus
  • Liquid Calories: Nut butters, oats, whey protein, banana and whole milk blended together make a quick 800+ calorie shake.
  • Healthy Fats: Almonds, walnuts, cashews, chia seeds, and pumpkin seeds are calorie-dense.
  • Dairy & Soy: Full-fat paneer, cheese, and tofu provide healthy fats and carbs.
  • Grains: Rice, sweet potatoes, oats, and quinoa are easy to digest in large quantities.
Non-Veg Focus
  • Whole Eggs: Rich in healthy fats and high-quality protein. Do not throw away the yolks!
  • Fatty Fish: Salmon and mackerel are high in calories and Omega-3 fatty acids.
  • Red Meats: Minced beef, mutton, and chicken thighs contain more calories than chicken breast.

Completing Protein

Veg Focus
  • Dairy: Low-fat paneer, Greek yogurt, curd, and cow milk are excellent vegetarian sources.
  • Soya Chunks: 100g of soya chunks contains an incredible ~52g of protein. Highly anabolic!
  • Legumes & Lentils: Chickpeas, kidney beans, black lentils, and edamame.
  • Whey Protein: Use 1-2 scoops of high-quality whey isolate or concentrate daily.
Non-Veg Focus
  • Chicken Breast: The gold standard. 100g of raw chicken breast contains ~23g protein and is super lean.
  • Egg Whites: Low calorie, high volume protein source. Add 5-6 egg whites to your breakfast.
  • Lean Fish: Tuna, cod, and tilapia are packed with high biological value protein.
